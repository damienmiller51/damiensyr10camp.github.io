Mt. Aspiring National Park

The Mt. Aspiring national park was established in 1964, as New Zealand's tenth national park. The park is 3,555 square
kilometeres and is located directly to the west of Lake Wanaka. The height of the Mt. Aspiring itself is 3,033
metres above sea level. The Haast Pass is found in the north-eastern part of the park.
