Lodge History

The Tititea Lodge up at Mt. Aspiring is a very important place for many Dunstan students, ex-students, teachers and others. The lodge
was originally a home for the Aspinall family, but they sold up when they built their new house further down the valley. This was in the
early 1970's. Dunstan have been using it from around 1973. Originally, all the land up the back of the lodge was farmland, and you
could graze cows on it, but when it changed hands the owners began to regenerate the bush in the area.

Originally, the Year 10 camps were 7 nights long, and they did a lot more things than we do these days. They would take large hikes up
to the Snowy (Glacier Burn) and go Bouldering for a day. But the teachers found that the students began to get a little bored, and so they
shortened it to 4 nights, and it has been like that ever since.
